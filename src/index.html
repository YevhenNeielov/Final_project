Tips for new cat owners

We have lots of advice for cat owners - including tips on understanding cat behavior; when or why to neuter and specific advice on how to look after a kitten during those first few weeks and months.

01.

Cats are independent

Survival is a solitary affair for cats; they are self-reliant for food, shelter, grooming and territory defence.

02.

Cats are territorial

A cat’s territory is an area that is defended, aggressively if required, against other cats. They use a range of methods to mark their territory.

03.

Cats are playful

Cats are intelligent and need both mental and physical stimulation. Play is important as it improves motor skills, provides brain training.
